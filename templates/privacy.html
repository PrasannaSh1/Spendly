Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and password. As you use the service, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — for example, to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and authenticate your account. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or email delivery services) solely to operate the application, and only to the extent necessary to provide the service to you.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
